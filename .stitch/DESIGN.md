# AuditUs Design System (V6 - Sunset Vibrant)

AuditUs is a friendly, high-energy social platform. The V6 design moves away from "cold tech" to a **Warm & Vibrant** aesthetic—high contrast, bold typography, and rounded organic shapes.

## Color System (Dual-Mode)

### Light Mode ("Solar Energy")
- **Canvas**: #FFFBF0 (Warm Cream/Bone)
- **Surface**: #FFFFFF (Pure White) with 40px radius.
- **Text**: #1A1A1A (Deep Charcoal)
- **Primary**: #FF5D01 (Electric Orange) - Energetic and non-corporate.
- **Secondary**: #00D1FF (Sky Blue) - For highlights.

### Dark Mode ("Midnight Pulse")
- **Canvas**: #0F0F10 (Bespoke Black)
- **Surface**: #1A1A1B (Charcoal Card) with 40px radius.
- **Text**: #F5F5F5 (Soft White)
- **Primary**: #FF7A00 (Glowing Mango)
- **Secondary**: #00D1FF (Sky Blue)

## Typography & Forms
- **Headlines**: **Plus Jakarta Sans** (ExtraBold/900). No tracking reduction, keep it open and friendly.
- **Body**: **Outfit** (Medium/500). Rounded and modern.
- **Radius**: Global **40px** (Organic rounded feel).

## Visual Elements
- **Cards**: Soft floating shadows (Blur 40px, Opacity 10%). No sharp borders.
- **Buttons**:
  - Oversized, ultra-rounded pills.
  - Primary uses a subtle "Sunset" gradient (Orange to Coral).
- **Icons**: Soft-cornered custom icons (No thin techy lines).
- **Vibe**: Friendly, energetic, social, and "alive".

## Interactions
- **Bounce Motion**: Use `spring` physics with high damping (stiffness: 300, damping: 20).
- **Haptic Colors**: Success states use Mint/Teal instead of clinical green.
